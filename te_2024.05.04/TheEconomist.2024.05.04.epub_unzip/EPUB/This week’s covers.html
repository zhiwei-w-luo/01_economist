In most of the world this week we lead with a dark and prophetic warning from Emmanuel Macron. In an interview with The Economist, France’s president warned that Europe’s elites are gripped by the same fatal complacency. He also spoke of the mountain of work ahead to make Europe safe. But he is bedevilled by unpopularity at home and poor relations with Germany. Like other gloomy visionaries, he faces the risk that his message is ignored.
